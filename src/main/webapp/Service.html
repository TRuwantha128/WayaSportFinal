Services, Activities & How Students/Stakeholders Can Use PEU
  • The PEU provides access to sports facilities: indoor stadiums, outdoor grounds/fields, courts (basketball, volleyball, etc.), fitness and recreation facilities.
  • Students (or outsiders, depending on policy) can apply to book or reserve university indoor stadiums, playgrounds, courts, etc. via specific forms (e.g. Application for Reservation of University Indoor Stadium, Application for Reservation of University Ground/Indoor Stadium/Volleyball Court / Basketball Court (Outdoor)).
  • The PEU organizes regular programmes: inter faculty freshers championships, inter faculty championships, national and international inter university games, fitness programmes, and more.
  • Awards & recognition: They hold a Colours Awarding Ceremony annually to recognize outstanding student athletes.
